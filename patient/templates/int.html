Intensive Care Unit


  • ICU has a total of six beds which are utilized for our ICU and step down patients. (Step down means when a patient is stepped down from ICU, but not ready to go to the Medical-Surgical Unit).

  • The ICU staff monitor up to 15 remote telemetries through a central monitoring systems for patients on the medical surgical floor

  • ICU Clinical staff encourages visits by family and friends to promote healing and family involvement in our patient care.

  • The ICU is staffed with experienced Registered Nurses (RNs), Respiratory Therapists and Telemetry Technicians/Certified Nurse Assistants (CNAs).

  • The ICU Medical Director is Marcella Abuid, MD who is trained as a specialist Pulmonogist/Intensivist.

  • The ICU offers a multi-disciplinary approach (RN, MD, Respiratory Therapy, Dieticians, Physical Therapy) to provide high quality medical care to ICU patients.
