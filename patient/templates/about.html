DELTA HOSPITAL


Delta Hospital provides general medical and surgical care for inpatient, outpatient, and emergency room patients, and participates in the Medicare and Medicaid programs.
The Services we provide include Diabetes Education, Diagnostic Radiology, Inpatient Surgery, Outpatient Surgery, Physical Therapy, Occupational Therapy, Intensive Care and much more...

Delta Hospital is now a Tobacco Free Campus

As an organization committed to the health of its employees and the community we serve, all Delta Hospital properties are now tobacco free environments.
As of June 20, 2016, DH has officially put into place a tobacco free policy. This policy applies to all staff, visitors, vendors, patients and their families.
What this means is that there will be no smoking or chewing of tobacco properties within the facilities or property of DH.
