Diabetes Educators

The Delta Hospital Dietitians and Diabetes Educators offer education and support to individuals and their family members who require assistance with nutrition-related diagnoses. We provide services to both inpatients and outpatients throughout the hospital and community. Services are provided by Registered Dietitian/Nutritionists (RD/RDN) and Certified Diabetes Educators (CDE).

Outpatient Services

Nutrition in Health & Disease:

The DCMH team of nutrition professionals offers one-on-one and group sessions to aid individuals who are working to manage their nutrition-related issues with health and disease.

Weight Management

We offer individual and group weight management sessions.

Diabetes Management

Diabetes is a manageable disease. With proper care and education, people with diabetes live healthy lives. Schedule an appointment to learn how to avoid the complications of this disease. We are continually expanding our services to meet this growing need in our community. Our Diabetes Support Group, ongoing for more than 20 years, meets at 2:30 p.m. on the second Monday of each month. The group is led by Dietitians and Diabetes Educators. Topics are generated by requests from the group. There is no charge for this program.
